📖 TaxClicker Pro™ 98 — Help Guide
✕

🏢 GETTING STARTED

Welcome to TaxClicker Pro™ 98, the premier Australian accounting firm idle simulator!

You start as a solo accountant in a garage. Your goal: build the most powerful accounting empire in Australia and outpace the Big Four firms.

Click "LODGE FINANCIALS & TAX RETURNS" (or press Spacebar) to earn revenue. Use that revenue to hire staff, buy equipment, and acquire clients.

📊 TABS EXPLAINED

TabWhat it does
📊 DashboardOverview of your cash, revenue per second, lifetime earnings, industry rank, and owned assets.
🛒 UpgradesBuy office equipment. Desks and calculators boost your click power. Printers, copiers, servers, and meeting rooms generate passive income.
👔 StaffHire staff from Work Experience Kids to the legendary Max Wellington. Staff generate passive revenue automatically.
📁 ClientsAcquire client accounts — Sole Traders, Pty Ltd businesses, ASX-listed companies, and Government Contracts. Clients generate passive revenue.
📈 SalesBuild a sales team to multiply ALL revenue (clicks + passive). Led by CRO Jake Harper. Each sales hire adds to your multiplier.
⚔️ RivalsLive leaderboard against PwC, KPMG, Deloitte and EY. Your rank is shown in the status bar.

💰 EARNING REVENUE

There are two ways to earn:

Clicking: Each click earns your base click value (boosted by desks, calculators, and hybrid staff like the CFO and Max Wellington).

Passive income: Staff, office equipment, and clients all earn revenue per second automatically — even while you're reading this help guide!

⚔️ RIVAL FIRMS

Four Big Four rivals compete against you:

RivalAggression
🟠 PwC AustraliaModerate — steady growth
🔵 KPMGCautious — slower start
🟢 DeloitteAggressive — fastest grower!
🟡 EYModerate — watch out mid-game

Rivals grow faster as your firm grows. During economic crises, rival growth slows — use these windows to overtake them!

The EOFY Rush (End of Financial Year) triggers automatically in Q4, giving 3x earnings for 30 seconds.

🐐 MAX WELLINGTON — THE ULTIMATE ACCOUNTANT

Once your firm reaches $500,000 lifetime revenue, the legendary Max Wellington becomes available for hire.

He costs $1,000,000 but delivers:

BenefitValue
Passive income+$500/sec
Click power+$200 per click
Rival debuffAll rival growth reduced 50%
Corner officeGolden office appears on floor plan

Max Wellington is the key to dominating the leaderboard. Save up!

🦈 JAKE HARPER — CHIEF REVENUE OFFICER

Once your firm reaches $250,000 lifetime revenue, Jake Harper becomes available on the Sales tab.

He costs $750,000 and adds a +2.0x sales multiplier — applied on top of the base multiplier from your other sales hires.

The Sales Team works differently from other upgrades. Instead of adding flat income, each sales hire multiplies ALL your revenue — both clicks and passive income.

Sales HireMultiplier per hireCost
📞 Cold Caller+0.1x$500
🤝 Business Dev Manager+0.15x$3,000
📋 Account Manager+0.2x$12,000
📊 Sales Director+0.3x$60,000
🦈 Jake Harper (CRO)+2.0x$750,000

Example: With a base of 1.0x, plus 3 Cold Callers (+0.3x) and Brad (+2.0x), your total multiplier is 3.3x — all clicks and passive income are tripled!

The game ends when your firm reaches $1,000,000,000 (one billion dollars) in lifetime revenue.

When you hit $1B, a win screen appears showing:

You can copy your result to share with other players and challenge them to beat your time! After winning you can restart for a new speed run.
